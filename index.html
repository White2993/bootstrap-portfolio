About Me

Hello, my name is Zackary White. I've been living in Nashville for over 20 years. I love my town, and when I start the job search I plan on staying local if I'm able.

I'll be honest; coding didn't always interest me. Actually up until the point that I started my bootcamp I wondered if I'd made a huge mistake. A very expensive mistake. But at least for now I can say that it wasn't. Quite the opposite. With all of the frustrations and looming dead lines, there's something about coding I find a bit peaceful and charming.

With that being said, I'm very much looking forward to collaborating with other people and contribute to something bigger than myself. If you have any questions, let me know, or check out my Github or LinkedIn. Thanks.
